Project 1

Brief Description of Project 1: Samuel Houston My first project focuses on Samuel Houston, an important American politician and military leader. Born on March 2, 1793, he played a key role in the Texas Revolution, leading the victory at the Battle of San Jacinto in 1836. Houston was the first president of the Republic of Texas and supported its annexation into the U.S. The project highlights key facts about his life and impact on Texas history.
